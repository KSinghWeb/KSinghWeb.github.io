Let Me Explain: Politics

Now politics is a tricky subject to navigate. While I don’t particularly care what our politicians are lying about, it’s nice to keep up with the news a bit to somewhat understand what your drunk MAGA uncle is complaining about. Scrolling through social media especially makes me a bit uncomfortable seeing how many people treat politics as a game. It has become a sport for people who hate sports. There are a few telltale signs and mannerisms to determine someone’s ideology online. Now I know what you’re thinking: “Gee man isn’t stereotyping kinda bad?” Rest assured, this is only generalizing.
